# Hungry Fish

A survival game built with JavaScript and Phaser.

## Premise
You are a small fish in dangerous waters. A school of predators is closing in, drooling toxic saliva at the thought of a meal. Your only defense is to satisfy their hunger by distracting them with your limited food supply before they reach you.

## Play Now
[Click here to play the game!](https://shahvishrut.github.io/hungry-fish/)

## Controls
* **W / S**: Move Up / Down
* **Space**: Shoot Food

## Gameplay
* **Feed the Predators**: Shoot food to satisfy the fish chasing you.
* **Ammo Management**: You have a limited amount of food. Each shot reduces your stock.
* **Refill**: Your stock only replenishes when you successfully hit/feed a fish.
* **Hazards**: 
    * **Poisonous Saliva**: Touching saliva loses **1 Life**.
    * **Fish Collision**: If a fish reaches you, it is **Instant Death**.
